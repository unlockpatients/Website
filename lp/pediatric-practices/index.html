Most practices begin receiving new patient inquiries within the first 1-2 weeks of launching campaigns. It typically takes 30-60 days to see the full impact as we optimize targeting and messaging based on real performance data.

Our management fees vary based on the scope of your campaigns and your growth goals. During your free demo, we'll discuss your specific situation and provide a transparent breakdown of costs including both our management fee and recommended ad spend.

Do you work with pediatric practices specifically?

Yes — we specialize in patient acquisition for medical practices, with deep expertise in pediatrics. We understand the unique dynamics of marketing to families, seasonal trends in pediatric care, and the compliance requirements of healthcare advertising.

What makes you different from other marketing agencies?

Unlike general marketing agencies, we focus exclusively on patient acquisition for medical practices. We don't just run ads — we provide a complete growth system including lead nurturing, appointment booking automation, and real-time reporting. Many of our clients came to us after working with general agencies that didn't deliver results. Our specialized focus on healthcare means we understand what works for practices like yours. We're happy to do a free audit of your current campaigns during the demo.

How do you track results?

You'll have access to a real-time dashboard showing every lead, appointment booked, cost per lead, and return on investment. We also provide weekly and monthly reports with insights and optimization recommendations from your dedicated account manager.
