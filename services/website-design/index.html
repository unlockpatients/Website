The problem

A pediatric site has 8 seconds to earn a phone call.

Most pediatric websites we audit are pretty, slow, and pointless. Stock photos of strangers' kids. A six-tab navigation. A phone number hidden three clicks deep. By the time a parent finds the booking button, they've already called a competitor.

We design backwards from the booking. Big phone number above the fold. New-patient form on every page. Clear answers to the four questions every parent has before they call.
