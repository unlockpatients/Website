Alex Langone

Founder and Account Manager

As a University of Miami student with 9 years of experience working in social media, including with prominent brands, I noticed my mother’s pediatric practice needed help with its online presence. With the office manager’s busy schedule and doctors focused on patient care, I stepped in to improve the practice's visibility. This led to more patient reviews, increased website traffic, new patients, and a stronger community where families felt more connected with the practice.


Inspired by these results, I founded a social media management company for pediatric offices. I help practices create and maintain a strong online presence that attracts and retains patients, allowing them to focus on patient care while I handle their industry-leading digital visibility.


-Alex

We will have achieved success when every pediatric practice we partner with can abundantly support their team, enjoy ample time for personal life, and consistently deliver transformative care to their communities. Ultimately, it’s not about us; it’s about you, your practice, and your patients.
